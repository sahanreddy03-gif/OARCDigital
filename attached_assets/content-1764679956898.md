## Client overview

Established in 2019, cricket-betting.com caters to cricket lovers worldwide, offering detailed match predictions backed by thorough analyses and expert insights. Beyond the odds, cricket-betting.com delves into the narratives behind each run, wicket, and boundary. With a team of seasoned experts analysing past performances, player forms, and pitch conditions, cricket-betting.com delivers comprehensive match predictions.

## The challenge

Cricket-betting.com faced a challenge with an outdated website user experience, impacting conversion rates. Additionally, the cluttered presence of affiliate links hindered the delivery of the core value proposition – expert-written, data-backed cricket predictions.

![mobile application development and ux and ui development](https://bullshark.digital/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Main-image-1-compressed-1-1024x513.webp)

## The solution

We designed a mobile-first web application with strategic elements to optimise Cricket Betting’s conversion rates:

### Match Pods

Redesigned match pods displaying odds with a compact view optimised for mobile, ensuring easy scrolling for users with optimal information hierarchy.

![](https://bullshark.digital/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Affiliate-Links-2-1-compressed-1024x964.webp)

### Affiliate Links

Strategically relocated affiliate links to strike a balance between directing traffic and maintaining user experience.

### Promotional Areas

Key website locations dedicated to affiliate highlights, maximising click-through rates.
